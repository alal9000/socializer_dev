About Foodie Friend

foodie friend is like a bulletin board for foodie meetups. Create an event, supply a pic and connect with other foodies in your area. Eat, meet, explore with Foodie Friend.

For Hosts

Create an event at the top, supply event details including a description of the event, add a profile pic and your away!

For Guests

Select an event on the homepage, provide a description of yourself, add a profile image and your away!
